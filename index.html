Privacy Policy

PipeFit Pro: Offset Calculator
Effective Date: February 8, 2026
Summary: PipeFit Pro does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All calculations are performed locally on your device.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how PipeFit Pro: Offset Calculator ("the App"), developed by Manh Hung ("Developer", "we", "us"), handles user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any information. The App:

  • Does not require user registration or login
  • Does not collect personal information (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • Does not collect device information or identifiers
  • Does not collect usage analytics or statistics
  • Does not collect location data
  • Does not use cookies or tracking technologies

3. Data Storage

The App stores your preferences (such as preferred units of measurement) locally on your device using standard device storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not accessible to the Developer or any third party
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the App or clearing App data

4. Internet & Network Access

PipeFit Pro does not require an internet connection to function. The App works entirely offline. All pipe fitting calculations, reference data, and tools operate locally on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services such as:

  • Analytics tools (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media SDKs
  • Crash reporting services

6. Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, we do not share any data with third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is a professional tool designed for pipefitters and plumbers. It is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

8. Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions:

  • Clipboard access: Only when you explicitly long-press to copy a calculation result
  • Share: Only when you explicitly choose to share a calculation result

No permissions are used to collect or transmit data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
